# Mental Health & Psychiatry

The Synalux Mental Health & Psychiatry module is designed to streamline clinical workflows, enhance patient care, and optimize billing processes for mental health professionals. Below are detailed descriptions of the key features included in this module:

## Clinical Templates
Streamline documentation with a suite of pre-formatted templates specifically tailored for psychiatric evaluations, psychotherapy sessions, Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT) or Cognitive Processing Therapy (CPT) protocols, and safety plans. These templates ensure consistency and completeness in patient records.

## Billing Codes
Effortlessly manage billing by integrating standard codes such as 90791 for psychiatric evaluations, 90834/90837 for therapy sessions of 45 or 60 minutes respectively, and 99214 for medication management. The system automatically applies the correct codes to ensure accurate reimbursement.

## Outcome Measures
Monitor patient progress using a range of validated outcome measures including PHQ-9 (Depression), GAD-7 (Anxiety), PTSD-5 (Post-Traumatic Stress Disorder), and BDI-II (Beck Depression Inventory II). These assessments are auto-scored, with trend tracking to provide clear insights into therapeutic outcomes.

## Medication Tracking
Maintain detailed medication records for each patient, including prescriptions, dose changes, side effects, and potential drug interactions. This feature helps in managing medication adherence and minimizing adverse events, supporting comprehensive care management.

## Trauma Therapy (CPT)
Implement evidence-based trauma therapy with a 12-session protocol that includes stuck point logs to track challenges during treatment and impact statement drafts to aid in processing traumatic experiences. This module is designed to facilitate the delivery of effective trauma-focused therapies.

## Crisis Protocol
Ensure safety and preparedness for crises through features such as urgent message flags, customizable safety plan templates, and integration with crisis hotlines. These tools help providers to quickly address critical situations and ensure patient well-being.

## Telehealth
Leverage seamless telehealth capabilities with integrated support for platforms like Zoom, including consent tracking to comply with legal requirements and session recording options when consented by the patient. This feature enables flexible delivery of care regardless of physical location.

## Insurance
Manage insurance authorizations efficiently by tracking session limits for insurers such as Anthem BCBS, Aetna, and Cigna Behavioral Health. The module ensures compliance with insurance policies and helps in maximizing coverage while minimizing administrative burden.
