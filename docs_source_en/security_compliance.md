# Security & Compliance

## HIPAA Compliance

Full HIPAA technical safeguard implementation including AES-256 encryption at rest, TLS 1.3 in transit, automatic 15-minute session timeouts, and comprehensive audit logging.

## Access Controls

Role-Based Access Control (RBAC) with server-side enforcement. Principle of least privilege ensures users access only data required for their role across all modules.

## Audit Trail

Immutable audit log of all data access, modifications, and administrative actions. Supports compliance audits, breach investigation, and regulatory reporting requirements.

## BAA Support

Business Associate Agreement available for Enterprise tier customers. Covers all Synalux cloud services and subprocessor relationships for full HIPAA compliance.
