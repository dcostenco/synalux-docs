## What you get

### Register
3-panel layout (categories + product grid + order ticket). Barcode scanning. Weight-based pricing. Open-price items. Seat assignment. Course firing. Age verification for alcohol.

### Kitchen Display (KDS)
4-column ticket board. Bump, recall, void. All-day count shows total pending per item. Color-coded by order type (dine-in blue, takeout orange, delivery purple). Supabase Realtime — tickets appear instantly.

### Payment
6 methods: card (Stripe Terminal NFC/chip/swipe), cash (with CAD penny rounding), gift card, house account, split check (even or custom), bar tab (pre-auth). Post-payment tip via QR. Cash discount / dual pricing. Chargeback evidence auto-pack.

### Tables
Interactive floor plan with positioned table widgets. Click to open order. Status colors (available/occupied/reserved). Table merge, split-back, transfer. QR table ordering — guests scan and order from their phone.

### Reports
8 report tabs: Sales (hourly heatmap), PMIX (product mix), Menu Engineering (Star/Dog/Puzzle/Workhorse quadrant), Speed of Service (per-station ticket times), Server Performance (leaderboard), Payments (mix chart), Voids/Comps, Labor. CSV export on every report.

### Staff & Labor
PIN authentication. 7-level RBAC (cashier → host → server → bartender → supervisor → manager → admin). Clock in/out. Break tracking (meal + rest, CA §226.7 compliant). Predictive scheduling alerts (overtime, clopening, minor labor). Tip pooling (FLSA-enforced, 3 distribution modes). Tip credit compliance.

### Online Ordering
Customer-facing responsive menu. Light theme. Category filters. Cart with tax calculation. Orders go straight to KDS. QR table ordering for dine-in guests.

### Loyalty & Marketing
Enroll members. Points + tiers (bronze/silver/gold). Check-in. Referral program. Email/SMS campaigns with TCPA consent tracking. Reputation management (auto-prompt Google/Yelp reviews).

### Inventory
Add items with SKU, unit, cost, threshold. Stock adjustment. Physical stocktake mode (bulk count). Low-stock and out-of-stock alerts. Vendor management with payment terms.

### Compliance
Age verification (alcohol). RBS certification tracking with expiry alerts. Alcohol incident logging (refusal, ID check, cutoff). IRS Form 8027 tip reporting. CCPA/GDPR data deletion API. Tax-exempt sales toggle. Audit trail on every action.

### Back Office
End-of-day with denomination counter, cash management (paid-in/out), tip pool distribution, GL journal export. 12 printable operational forms (cash count sheet, void authorization, BEO, prep list, etc.). Multi-location dashboard with per-venue KPIs and consolidated P&L.

### Integrations
Stripe Terminal (card payments), DoorDash/Uber Eats/Grubhub (delivery webhook), OpenTable/Google Reserve/Yelp (reservation webhook), QuickBooks/Xero (GL journal sync), SendGrid (email receipts), Twilio (SMS receipts + phone ordering), Gusto/ADP (payroll export).

## Offline mode

Register, cash payments, KDS, receipt printing, and table management work without internet. Orders queue locally and sync automatically when connection returns. Card payments batch via Stripe offline mode.
