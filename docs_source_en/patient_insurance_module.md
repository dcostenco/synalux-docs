# Patient Insurance Module

## Insurance Verification

Real-time eligibility verification for major payers. Validates coverage, copays, deductibles, and remaining benefits before appointments to reduce claim denials.

## Policy Management

Store and manage multiple insurance policies per patient with primary/secondary/tertiary designations. Tracks policy effective dates, group numbers, and subscriber information.

## Prior Authorization

Automated prior auth requests with status tracking. Generates appeal letters from templates when authorizations are denied, streamlining the appeals process.

## Claims Tracking

End-to-end claim lifecycle tracking from submission through remittance. Denial management workflow with automated resubmission for common rejection codes.
