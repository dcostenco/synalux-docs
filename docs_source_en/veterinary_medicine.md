# Veterinary Medicine

## Clinical Records

The Synalux Veterinary module provides comprehensive animal health records with breed, species, weight tracking, and vaccination history. Each patient record supports multi-species intake (canine, feline, equine, exotic) with species-specific vital ranges and growth charts. Records include owner/guardian linkage for billing and communication.

## Veterinarian & Vet Tech Roles

Two dedicated RBAC roles support veterinary workflows. The Veterinarian role provides full clinical access including exam documentation, surgical reports, prescription management, animal pathology, and diagnostic imaging review. The Vet Technician role covers animal intake, vitals collection, lab preparation, and treatment administration with appropriate access restrictions.

## Clinical Exams & Surgical Notes

Structured exam templates capture chief complaint, physical examination findings, diagnostic plan, and treatment recommendations. Surgical note templates include pre-op assessment, anesthesia protocol, procedure details, and post-operative care instructions with automated follow-up scheduling.

## Prescription Management

Veterinary-specific prescription workflows support species-appropriate dosing, compounding pharmacy integration, and controlled substance tracking. Drug interaction checking accounts for species-specific contraindications and weight-based dosing calculations.

## Vaccination & Wellness Schedules

Automated vaccination scheduling based on species, breed, and age with support for core and non-core vaccine protocols. Wellness visit reminders are sent to pet owners via the Patient Portal with species-appropriate preventive care recommendations.

## Diagnostic Imaging

DICOM-compatible diagnostic imaging review for radiographs, ultrasound, and specialty imaging. Images are stored securely within the patient record with annotation tools for clinical documentation and referral communication.

## Billing & Insurance

Support for veterinary-specific billing including wellness plans, procedure bundles, and pet insurance claim submission. Integrates with the platform's Stripe-powered payment processing for point-of-service collection and payment plans.
