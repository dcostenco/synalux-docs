# Lab Orders & Results Module

The Synalux Lab Orders & Results Module is designed to streamline laboratory testing processes, ensuring efficient management of lab orders, tracking of results, and comprehensive analysis of medical tests across various categories.

## Lab Orders
Effortlessly manage lab orders with detailed vendor tracking (Quest, LabCorp, in-house) and categorize them by priority levels: routine, urgent, or stat. This feature allows healthcare providers to prioritize patient care effectively while maintaining clear oversight over all laboratory requests.

## Result Tracking
Receive comprehensive individual test results complete with reference ranges and automatic abnormal flags (low/high/critical). This ensures that healthcare providers are immediately aware of any out-of-range results, facilitating timely medical interventions.

## Categories
The module supports a wide array of categories including Hematology, Chemistry, Lipid, Liver, Thyroid, Vitamin, Inflammation, and Coagulation. This categorization helps in organizing test orders and results, making it easier for providers to navigate through patient data.

## Abnormal Alerts
Automatically flag out-of-range results for quick attention, such as elevated TSH levels or low Vitamin D concentrations. These alerts are crucial for early diagnosis and prompt treatment, enhancing patient outcomes.

## iPLEDGE Labs
Facilitate monthly Accutane monitoring by automating CBC, CMP, lipid panel, and LFTs tests along with trend tracking. This feature is essential for patients on isotretinoin therapy to ensure compliance and safety through continuous health monitoring.

## Pre-Surgical
Ensure pre-surgical clearance by managing INR, PT, glucose, and A1C levels specifically for dental implants and surgical procedures. This category aids in preparing patients medically before invasive procedures.

## Medication Monitoring
Support medication management with SSRI thyroid checks, stimulant lipid panels, and biologic baseline panels. These features help monitor patient response to medications and adjust treatments as necessary.

## Order Lifecycle
Track the complete lifecycle of lab orders through six stages: Ordered → Collected → Sent → Received → In Progress → Resulted → Reviewed. This detailed tracking ensures transparency and accountability in laboratory processes.

## Vendor Integration
Seamlessly route lab orders to Quest Diagnostics and LabCorp, with plans for future electronic result import capabilities. This integration streamlines the workflow, reducing manual data entry and minimizing errors.

## Diagnosis Linking
Attach ICD-10 codes to lab orders for thorough medical necessity documentation. This feature ensures compliance with regulatory standards and supports accurate billing and insurance claims processes.
