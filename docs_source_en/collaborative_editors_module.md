# 📝 Collaborative Editor Suite

## Real-Time Document & Spreadsheet Editing
Experience a true Google Docs-like collaborative environment natively embedded within the Synalux platform. Say goodbye to external windows and disjointed workflows—our suite brings rich text drafting, spreadsheet analytics, and clinical logic together into one unified, HIPAA-compliant workspace.

![Collaborative Editor Suite](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/dcostenco/synalux-docs/main/docs/demo/22_collaboration_suite.png)

## Key Features

![Collaborative Documents Editor](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/dcostenco/synalux-docs/main/docs/demo/22_collaboration_suite.png)

### 1. Collaborative Documents
- **Rich Text Formatting:** A powerful, intuitive toolbar supporting comprehensive text formatting, dynamic embedded images, and complex table management.
- **Cursor Presence & Multiplayer:** Work simultaneously with other providers and technicians. See exactly where your team members are typing with color-coded, labeled presence avatars.
- **Actionable Tooltips:** Intelligently-triggered medical auto-completion popups enhance typing speed and reduce clinical documentation errors.

### 2. Clinical Spreadsheets
Experience comprehensive tabular data management with our dedicated clinical spreadsheets interface. 

![Collaborative Spreadsheets](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/dcostenco/synalux-docs/main/docs/demo/23_collab_sheets.png)

- **Split-View Integration:** View patient vitals, lab results, and medications natively alongside your clinical narrative. 
- **Live Computations:** Utilize rich analytical capabilities to dynamically calculate dosage limits, historical trajectory, and growth percentiles without leaving the portal.
- **Dynamic Charts:** Render beautiful visual summaries of raw spreadsheet data with deep customizable axes.

### 3. Medical Presentations
Build robust, media-rich visual case studies and grand rounds presentations using our native slides editor.
- **Slide Canvas:** Deep integration with 3D anatomical models and DICOM imaging right on your slide canvas.
- **Media Sidebars:** Rapidly import videos, x-rays, and vector shapes via the persistent left sidebar.
- **Audience Mode:** Cast presentations natively through LiveKit Telehealth integration directly to conference attendees.

### 3. Persistent AI Assistant (Floating Chat)
- Context-aware intelligence follows you across documents and spreadsheets.
- Instantly request clinical translations, automated draft summarizations, or context-driven medical code mappings directly from highlighted text.

### 4. Seamless Version History
- Retain complete oversight with a strict, immutable version history timeline.
- A fully HIPAA-compliant audit trail logs every keystroke, insert, and deletion, ensuring undeniable clinical accountability and rapid rollback capability.
