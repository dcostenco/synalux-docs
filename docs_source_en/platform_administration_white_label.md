# Platform Administration & White-Label

## Multi-Tenant Architecture

Each practice operates in an isolated workspace with independent data, users, and configurations. Cross-tenant data access is architecturally impossible through row-level security.

## White-Label Branding

Customize logo, colors, email templates, and domain for complete brand ownership. Patients see your practice brand throughout the entire experience.

## User Management

Invite, remove, and manage team members with granular role assignments. Activity audit logs track all administrative actions for HIPAA compliance.

## Feature Flags

Enable/disable platform modules per workspace. Practices only see features relevant to their specialty and subscription tier for a streamlined experience.
