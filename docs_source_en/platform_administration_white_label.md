# ⚙️ Platform Administration & White-Label

Synalux v11.1 Elite provides high-level "Command and Control" for practice owners and enterprise administrators. Manage multiple branches, customize your brand identity, and enforce granular security policies across your entire organization from a single, unified dashboard.

---

## 🏢 Enterprise Branch Network Manager
Scale your practice globally without losing control.
*   **Multi-Region Management:** Manage US, European, and Canadian branches with localized currency and compliance rules.
*   **Hierarchy at a Glance:** Roll-up financial and operational metrics from individual clinics to the HQ dashboard.
*   **Instant Provisioning:** Deploy a new branch with pre-configured clinical templates and billing rules in seconds.

## 🎨 Professional White-Labeling
Make Synalux look and feel like your own proprietary platform.
*   **Dynamic Branding:** Upload your practice logo and set your primary brand colors (e.g., #4f46e5).
*   **Themed Portals:** Your patient portal and staff dashboard instantly adopt your clinical brand identity.
*   **Custom Domain Support:** Host your clinical portal on your own domain (e.g., portal.yourclinic.com).

## 🛠️ Dynamic Module Configuration
Customize the platform for your specific clinical needs.
*   **Drag-and-Drop Modules:** Enable only the modules your practice needs (e.g., disable Dental for an ABA clinic).
*   **Screen Builders:** Rename buttons, hide unnecessary table columns, and override standard UI text.
*   **Role Personalization:** Create specialized dashboards for different staff levels (Front Desk vs. Senior Doctor).

## 🔐 Advanced Security & Feature Toggling
Enforce the principle of "Least Privilege" across your team.
*   **Employee Overrides:** Disable specific features (like "Export to PDF") for individual staff members.
*   **API-Level Blocking:** Security policies are enforced at the server layer, not just in the UI.
*   **IP Whitelisting:** Restrict clinical access to known office or VPN IP addresses for maximum security.

## 🛡️ Break-Glass & Compliance Audit
Maintain absolute accountability in emergency situations.
*   **Break-Glass Protocol:** Grant temporary emergency access to patient records with one-click authorization.
*   **Triple-Logging:** Every admin action, break-glass event, and PHI access is triple-logged and immutable.
*   **Audit Vault:** Export 7-year compliant audit logs for HIPAA or insurance inspections instantly.
